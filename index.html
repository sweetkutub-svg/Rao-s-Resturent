Our Story

Crafting Memories
One Plate at a Time

"Every spice has a story, every recipe a soul. We don't just cook food — we carry forward generations of love onto every plate we serve."

Rao's began as a humble dream on Park Street in 1995 — a single kitchen, a handful of recipes, and an unwavering belief that food cooked with intention tastes unlike anything else. Three decades later, that belief still drives every flame in our kitchen.
